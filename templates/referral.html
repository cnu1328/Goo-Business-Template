Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to commonly asked questions about our referral program

How does the referral program work?

Simply share your unique referral link with friends and family, and when they sign up using your link, you'll receive a discount on your next purchase.

Is there a limit to how many referrals I can make?

No, there is no limit to the number of referrals you can make. The more people you refer, the more discounts you'll earn!

How do I receive my referral discount?

Once your referral signs up using your unique link and makes a purchase, your discount will be automatically applied to your next order at checkout.

How can I access my unique referral link?

Your unique referral link can be found in your account dashboard. Simply log in to your account and navigate to the referral section to copy your link.

Can I refer myself to receive the discount?

No, self-referrals are not eligible for the referral discount. The referral program is designed to encourage you to share our platform with others.

Are there any restrictions on who I can refer?

Anyone is eligible to be referred, as long as they meet the eligibility criteria for signing up on our platform. However, please ensure that you are following our referral program terms and conditions.

How long is my referral link valid?

Your referral link does not expire, so you can share it with others at any time. However, please note that referral discounts may be subject to change based on our referral program policies.

What if my referral doesn't use my link to sign up?

If your referral signs up without using your unique link, unfortunately, you will not receive the referral discount. We recommend reminding them to use your link to ensure you receive credit for the referral.

Can I track the status of my referrals?

Yes, you can track the status of your referrals in your account dashboard. You'll be able to see how many referrals you've made and whether they have completed the sign-up process.
